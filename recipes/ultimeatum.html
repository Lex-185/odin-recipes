The Ulti-Meatum

A burger so rare that it's served only once in a century

Prepared by head chef Ajay Maldonaldo from the Grill Em' Up Truck, the Ulti-Meatum is a burger cooked inside another burger. A symbol of health.

Only one is given out per customer and anyone who doesn't eat one... is a chump.
