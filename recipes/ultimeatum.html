The Ulti-Meatum

A burger so rare that it's served only once in a century

Prepared by head chef Ajay Maldonaldo from the Grill Em' Up Truck, the Ulti-Meatum is a burger cooked inside a another burger. A symbol of health.

Only one is given out per customer.

Ingredients:

  • 2 types of cheese
  • Beef
  • Pastrami
  • Hamburger buns
