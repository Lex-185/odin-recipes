Mississippi Queen

Mississippi Queen? Why do you call it that?

It's an hallucinogenic drink.

You might not feel it's effects at first, but I assure you that it's extremely potent.

Ingredients:

  • Soy sauce
  • Kimchi
  • Clam juice
  • Shrimp
  • Scallops
  • Sashimi
  • Hot chilli peppers
  • Tomato juice(1l)
  • Hot sauce
  • Magic mushrooms(The secret ingredient)
  • Wasabi

Instructions:

The drink:

  1. Pour the tomato juice and hot sauce in a blender.
  2. Add soy, clam juice, wasabi and kimchi into the concoction.
  3. Grind the mushrooms.
  4. Add a few sprinkles of salt, pepper and mushrooms.
  5. In addition, chop and add the chopped peppers to the mix.
  6. Blend to your hearts content.

Rush hour in your intestines:

  1. Sear scallops at medium heat for 3 minutes.
  2. Saute shrimp and set aside.
  3. Bring the two together on a kebab stick and place in the drink along with a cocktail umbrella.
  4. Bottom's up, fellas.
